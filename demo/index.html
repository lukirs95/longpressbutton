Long Press Button DEMO

A lightwight module for adding long press functionality to your buttons

longpressbutton-min.js

Turn every button or element of the DOM into a long pressable button by linking the longpressbutton-min.js into your html head

<script src="./path-to-/longpressbutton-min.js" defer></script>

and add data-longpress="true" and data-longpress="time-in-ms" e.g.

<button data-longpress="1000">Press Me</button>
FIRE!
